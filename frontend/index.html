Effortless Core Features

Multi-Level Approval Workflow

Easily define complex approval sequences involving **multiple managers** or departments (Finance, Director), ensuring complete accountability at every step.

Flexible Conditional Rules

Configure dynamic rules, such as **percentage approval thresholds** (e.g., 60% of approvers) or mandates for specific roles (e.g., CFO approval for high-value claims).

Comprehensive Role Management

Assign and manage distinct roles for **Admin**, **Manager**, and **Employee**, with permissions precisely tailored to their responsibilities within the expense lifecycle.

Global Currency Support

Employees can submit claims in any currency. The system automatically handles conversions and displays amounts in the company's designated default currency for review.

Our Journey to ExpenseSync

ExpenseSync began in 2020 when a small team of finance experts and software engineers grew tired of manual, error-prone expense reporting processes. Our vision was simple: create an expense management platform that is **effortless for employees** and provides **total control and transparency for finance teams**. Through rigorous development focused on automated, multi-level approvals and robust global currency support, we launched ExpenseSync in 2023. Today, we stand as a testament to the belief that modern business expenses should be managed with speed, security, and simplicity, giving companies back invaluable time and resources.
