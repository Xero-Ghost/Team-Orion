Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the login page by clicking the "Forgot Password?" link. An email will be sent to you with instructions.

Who can use this system?

This system is designed for employees, managers, and administrators of a registered company to manage business expenses.

For Employees

How do I submit an expense?

Navigate to the "Submit Expense" page from your dashboard. Fill in all required fields, upload your receipt, and click "Submit".

What if my expense is rejected?

If an expense is rejected, you will receive a notification. You can view the reason in the expense details page. You may be able to edit and resubmit it after making the necessary corrections.

For Managers

How do I approve or reject expenses?

Go to the "Pending Approvals" page. You can review each expense and click the "Approve" or "Reject" button. You may be prompted to add a comment when rejecting.

For Admins

How do I add a new user?

From the Admin Dashboard, navigate to the "User Management" section. Click the "Add User" button and fill in the required details.

How do I configure approval rules?

Navigate to "Approval Rules" from the admin sidebar. Here you can create and manage the workflows for how expenses are approved based on amount or other criteria.
